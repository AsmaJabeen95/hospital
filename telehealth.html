TeleHealth

“Recover & Exercise At Home With TeleHealth Video Visits Physical Therapy and Therapeutic Fitness…”

Telehealth Video Visits are now an ideal option for many patients from HyperPhysio Services, especially with the spread of COVID-19 impacting so much of daily life. As healthcare providers, we will remain available to accommodate our patient’s needs for physical therapy and fitness classes. Your TeleHealth Video Visit sessions are conducted via live video with us, just like you would video chat via FaceTime or Skype. Patients report that they love the convenience and great care they receive via Video Visits. All you need is an internet connection and a computer, laptop, tablet or phone with camera and microphone. The process is very easy!

What are the benefits of Video Visits?

Have you ever wanted your physical therapist to teach you how to move better in your home? Do you have a difficult time getting to your appointments? People are attached to technology more than ever before. We live in an on-demand society. You want what you want, when you want it. If you need immediate care, and your time is limited, especially if your priority is exercise prescription and general education/guidance a TeleHealth Video Visit may be for you. This is the next best option to an in person visit.

Telehealth has many benefits, including:

  • It works
  • It promotes social distancing
  • It's accessible
  • It's convenient
  • It saves time and transportation

What can we treat with Video Visits?

With TeleHealth Video Visits we can treat many conditions through education, body awareness and therapeutic movement/exercise. People of all ages and who have a variety of symptoms and medical conditions may be candidates for Telehealth Video Visits. A few examples of conditions for which a PT can help you virtually include:

  • Balance
  • Postoperative care
  • Chronic pain management
  • Cancer rehabilitation
  • Other conditions your PT determines are appropriate
