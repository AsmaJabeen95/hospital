Cardiac Physiotherapy

" ----- "

Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation is an area of practice that focuses on the evaluation and treatment of individuals with acute and chronic heart and lung disease. At RA Physical Therapy, we work in conjunction with board-certified pulmonologists (i.e. lung specialists) to help individuals with chronic respiratory disorders gain control over their breathing and improve their pulmonary function. Cardiac rehabilitation is designed to help individuals recover from a heart attack or heart surgery, using education and training to increase physical fitness and promote healthy living through lifestyle modification to reduce the risk of future heart problems. People who may benefit include those with the following diagnoses:

  • Heart Attack
  • CAD
  • Angina
  • Heart Failure
  • S/P CABG
  • S/P Angioplasty
  • S/P Valve Replacement

Pulmonary rehabilitation benefits individuals with conditions such as:

  • COPD
  • Emphysema
  • Post Lung Surgery
  • Pulmonary Hypertension
  • Asthma
  • Pneumonia
Treatment focuses on:
  • Interval exercise training
  • Education on breathing techniques
  • Chest wall and thoracic mobility
  • Strengthening muscles important for posture and breathing
  • Education and teaching of airway clearance techniques
  • Aerobic and anaerobic activities
  • Education of appropriate exercise parameters
  • Reconditioning of the heart and lungs
