Orthopedic Physiotherapy

"Get back in the game faster, safer and better than before..."

We treat all types of orthopedic disorders, ranging from pain to weakness to stiffness and instability.

Our physical therapy treatments are in line with current practices and research and incorporate many different types of physical therapy, including hands-on manual therapy, therapeutic and functional exercise training, and movement retraining and pain management techniques.

Every patient undergoes a thorough evaluation and physical examination conducted by us. A detailed physical examination and review of your medical history allows us to create a personalized plan of care for you based on your condition, goals and schedule.

Once your examination and evaluation have been completed, we will help you understand your specific problem and explain your diagnosis and treatment plan.

Conditions Treated

We offers a broad array of physical therapy services to treat a number of orthopedic conditions and disorders including:

  • Sprains/strains
  • Tendinitis
  • Bursitis
  • Arthritis
  • Joint stiffness
  • Balance/coordination problems
  • Instability
  • Spinal/pelvic disorders
  • Shoulder/elbow pain
  • Knee pain
  • Foot/ankle pain
  • Complex musculoskeletal dysfunction
